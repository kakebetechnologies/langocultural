Lango Cultural Festival: Celebrating Heritage and Unity

The Lango Cultural Institution successfully hosted its annual Cultural Festival this weekend in Lira City, bringing together hundreds of community members from across the sub-region. The event featured traditional dances, music performances, storytelling sessions, and exhibitions of local crafts, emphasizing the importance of preserving Lango heritage for future generations.

Youth mentorship workshops were also conducted alongside the festival, providing young people with opportunities to learn traditional skills while promoting entrepreneurship and leadership in the community. Visitors had the chance to participate in hands-on cultural activities, taste traditional foods, and engage with elders to learn more about Lango history and rituals.

This year's festival also highlighted the institution's ongoing community development projects, including support for local schools, health outreach programs, and cultural preservation initiatives. The Lango Cultural Institution continues to bridge generations, ensuring that the rich traditions of the Lango people remain vibrant and celebrated.
